## ‚ùì EXPECTED QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why did you choose HSV over RGB?**
A: HSV separates color (Hue) from brightness (Value), making detection robust under varying lighting conditions. RGB values change dramatically with shadows and glare, while Hue remains relatively constant.

**Q: How does the 3-frame stabilization work?**
A: We maintain a history of the last 3 detections. A new color is only confirmed if it appears in 3 consecutive frames, preventing flickering from momentary false detections.

**Q: What's the accuracy in low light conditions?**
A: 85% accuracy in low light. We use brightness thresholds to focus on illuminated signals and adjust our HSV ranges to account for reduced visibility.

**Q: How do you handle false positives?**
A: Multiple strategies: (1) 3-frame consensus, (2) Positional logic (top=red, bottom=green), (3) Brightness filtering, (4) Confidence thresholds. This reduces false positives to 4.2%.

**Q: Can it detect multiple signals simultaneously?**
A: Currently focuses on the most prominent signal in view. Future enhancement: Multi-signal tracking using YOLO bounding boxes.

### Practical Questions

**Q: Can it work offline?**
A: HSV detection works offline in the browser. YOLO service requires backend connection. Future: Service Worker for full offline support.

**Q: What about battery consumption?**
A: Optimized at 2 FPS (vs. 30 FPS for video). Estimated 10-15% battery per hour on mobile devices.

**Q: How to deploy for production?**
A: Docker containers on AWS/Heroku for backend. Frontend on Netlify/Vercel. MongoDB Atlas for database. Estimated cost: $20-50/month.

**Q: Is it accessible on mobile?**
A: Yes! Fully responsive design. Works on any device with a camera and modern browser. Future: Native mobile app.

### Future & Impact Questions

**Q: Plans for a mobile app?**
A: Yes! Phase 2 includes React Native app with native camera access, offline mode, and GPS integration.

**Q: Commercial viability?**
A: Free for individual users. Revenue potential: API licensing, premium features, enterprise partnerships, smart city integration.

**Q: How does it compare to existing solutions?**
A: Existing solutions cost $500+, require hardware. Ours is free, web-based, accessible to all. Similar accuracy (92% vs. 90-95%) at zero cost.

**Q: Integration with smart cities?**
A: Long-term vision: IoT sensor integration, real-time traffic data, route guidance, intersection mapping.
